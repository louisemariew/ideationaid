About The Ideator


The Ideator is a tool where you can create idea boards to explore word connections. Enter a word on the board and click on nodes to expand them with further searches.
Alternatively, add more words to the board that aren't already connected to existing words in some way - the Ideator will find any common connections that come up!


You can filter your searches on the degree of connection and the number of word suggestions you would like.

Degree of connection is defined as:
  • 0 for lateral connections - those which are unusual and unexpected connections to the search word.
  • 0.25 for connections that are more lateral than literal in their connection to the search word.
  • 0.5 for connections that could be considered partially literal or lateral in their connection to the word.
  • 0.75 for connections that are more literal than lateral in their connection to the search word.
  • 1 for literal connections - those which are highly related and relevant to the search word.

We hope you enjoy using this tool! Click on the Idea Board tab to get started.
